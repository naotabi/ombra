Ombra. — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Ombra ("the app") is an Italian listening and shadowing practice app. This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: the app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Data stored on your device

The app stores the following information locally on your device only, so that it can function:

  • Your practice settings (course, speed, repeats, and similar preferences)
  • Your progress and practice statistics (streak, sessions, minutes)
  • Sentence reports and feedback you write inside the app

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to us or to any third party. Deleting the app deletes this data.

No accounts, no tracking

The app does not require an account, does not collect your name, email address, or location, and contains no analytics or tracking of your behavior.

Advertising

The free version of the app may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and use them to show ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy. Purchasing the ad-free upgrade removes advertisements entirely. The app itself does not share any of your practice data with advertisers.

External links

The app's optional "Support" section contains links to third-party websites and services, including referral links. Those websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Future changes

If we add features that change how data is handled — for example, an optional account for syncing progress between devices — this policy will be updated before the feature is released, and the changes will be announced in the app or in release notes. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
